AI-built parametric CAD

Describe a part →
a forge.

Tell an AI what you want to make and refine it in plain language. Instead of a one-off 3-D file you get a forge — a self-contained web app for that part, with just the controls that matter. Share it, and anyone can dial in their own version and export something to print.

The forges below are live — open one, change a parameter, download a part to print.

Faceted Planter

A planter, cup, or vase. Set the facets, taper, twist, and wall, toggle a drainage hole, then export a part to print — thin the wall and the forge flags that it won't print cleanly.
